FAQ

Common questions

Is TimeTally free like TomatoTimer? +

Yes, completely free. No account, no premium tier, no ads blocking the timer. Every feature — multiple tabs, YouTube tasks, text-to-speech, session ETA — works immediately in any browser.

Can I still use 25/5 Pomodoro blocks in TimeTally? +

Yes. Set any task to 25 minutes and add 5-minute break tasks — the Pomodoro technique works perfectly in TimeTally. The difference is you can also mix in longer or shorter blocks whenever you need to.

Does it work offline? +

Yes. Install TimeTally as a PWA from your browser — no app store required. Once installed, it runs fully offline and your saved task lists are always available.

Can I save my task lists? +

Yes. TimeTally saves your task lists automatically in your browser's local storage — no account needed. Your work, study, or workout routines are restored every time you open the app.

Does TimeTally track how many sessions I've completed? +

Yes. TimeTally tracks your session count, total time worked, current streak, and best streak per list. View your stats at any time from the Stats screen. TomatoTimer tracks none of this.
