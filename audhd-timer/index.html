FAQ

Common questions

What is AuDHD? +

AuDHD refers to people who are both autistic and have ADHD. The combination often creates a push-pull between the autistic need for predictable structure and the ADHD need for flexible stimulation. Productivity tools that are rigid or unpredictable often fail this community.

Can I see my full session plan before starting? +

Yes. All tasks are visible in the list at all times — their names, durations, and order. You know everything that's coming before you press play.

Can I turn off the audio announcements? +

Yes. Text-to-speech and sound effects are toggleable in settings. If you prefer a silent visual timer, turn off audio entirely — all the other features still work.

Is TimeTally free? +

Yes, completely. No account, no premium tier, no time limits. Every feature works immediately in your browser.
