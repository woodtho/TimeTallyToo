Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

TimeTally stores everything in your browser. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on our servers. We don't even have a server.

What data TimeTally stores

Your task lists, timer settings, session statistics, streaks, and preferences are saved in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

TimeTally does not use accounts, cookies set by the app itself, or any form of user tracking. Clearing your browser's site data for timetally.ca will erase your saved lists and settings.

Google AdSense

TimeTally displays ads served by Google AdSense. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your browsing activity across other sites. This is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings or by using the Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out tool.

European visitors (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, Google AdSense may serve non-personalized ads in your region. TimeTally itself does not process any personal data as defined by GDPR — there is no user account, no server-side storage, and no analytics data collected by us.

Analytics

TimeTally does not use Google Analytics or any other analytics service. We have no visibility into individual user behaviour, sessions, or identities.

Children

TimeTally does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app does not collect any information from any user.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the date above. Continued use of TimeTally after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact
