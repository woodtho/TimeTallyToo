How it works

Your chore list, timed and running in 2 minutes

No app to install. No account. Just your chores and a timer.

1

Add your chores

Type each task — "Dishes," "Vacuum living room," "Wipe counters," "Take out trash." Named exactly so you hear them called out clearly.

2

Set a time for each

Give each chore the time it usually takes — 10 minutes for dishes, 15 for vacuuming. Be honest. The ETA is only useful if the times are real.

3

See when you'll be done

The live ETA shows immediately: "Chores done by 12:15 PM." Knowing the end point makes starting easier.

4

Start and let it run

Each chore is announced by voice when it starts. When the timer ends, the next one begins automatically — no phone required.

FAQ

Common questions

Can I save my chore routine for next week? +

Yes. Your chore list saves automatically in your browser. Install TimeTally as a PWA and your saved routine is one tap away every time — no login, no sync, no friction.

Does it work while my phone screen is off? +

The timer continues running in the background on most browsers. Text-to-speech announcements may require the screen to be on, depending on your device's browser and audio settings.

Is it free? +

Yes. Completely free — no account, no premium tier, no time limits. Every feature works immediately in any browser.

Can kids use it for their chore list? +

Yes. No account required, no sign-up, no personal information collected. Hand them a phone or tablet with the chore list already loaded and let them go.
