# TimeTally Instagram Messaging

## Brand Positioning

TimeTally is a structured timer for people who want to stop deciding what to do next and just move through the list. It works for focus blocks, study sessions, workouts, routines, and guided sessions where timing, momentum, and clear next steps matter.

Core promise:

- Plan the session
- Start the timer
- Follow the list

## Audience Angles

### 1. Focus and productivity

For people who want less friction between planning and doing.

Message:
Turn a messy to-do list into a timed flow you can actually follow.

### 2. Study and routines

For students and self-directed learners.

Message:
Build your study blocks once, then let TimeTally guide the session.

### 3. Fitness and guided sessions

For circuits, mobility, stretching, recovery, and step-by-step workouts.

Message:
Set each interval, hit start, and move without watching the clock.

## Core Value Props

- Build multiple timed lists for different parts of your day
- Start, pause, skip, complete early, or restart without losing structure
- Follow task-by-task sessions with ETA and remaining time
- Add YouTube links as tasks for guided videos or follow-alongs
- Use beep and voice prompts so you always know what is next
- Keep the timer visible with picture-in-picture
- Track sessions, streaks, and time worked
- Keep data local in the browser

## Short Ad Hooks

- Your routine should run itself.
