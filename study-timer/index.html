FAQ

Common questions

How does the ETA bar work? +

TimeTally adds up the remaining time for every task in your current list and calculates a finish time — for example, "done at 4:15 PM." It updates live as tasks complete, so you always know exactly when your session will end.

Does TimeTally work on my phone while studying? +

Yes. TimeTally is a PWA — install it directly from your browser without going through an app store. It works on any phone or tablet, and your task lists are always available without logging in.

Can I have separate timers for different subjects? +

Yes. Each browser tab can hold a named list — Biology, Maths, Chemistry — with its own task list, settings, and streak count. Switch between subjects by switching tabs. Your progress in each is tracked independently.

Does it track study streaks? +

Yes. TimeTally tracks your current streak and best streak per subject list. Complete at least one full session each day to keep your streak going. Streaks are visible in the Stats screen for each list.

What if I want to change the task order mid-session? +

You can edit any task name or duration at any time — even while the timer is running. The ETA bar updates immediately to reflect the change. Tap any task to edit it inline.

Will my task lists be saved if I close the browser? +

Yes. TimeTally saves everything in your browser's local storage — no account needed. Your lists, settings, and streak data are automatically restored each time you open the app.
