FAQ

Common questions

Is TimeTally really free with no catch? +

Yes. Every feature is free — unlimited tabs, YouTube tasks, ETA, text-to-speech. No premium tier, no trial, no account. The site has Google Ads to cover hosting costs, but everything functional is unrestricted.

Does TimeTally work on desktop? +

Yes. TimeTally runs in any browser on any device — Windows, Mac, Linux, Android, iOS. No app to install. Forest App's desktop experience requires a separate browser extension that is not free.

Can I still use Pomodoro intervals with TimeTally? +

Yes. Add a 25-minute work task, a 5-minute break task, repeat as many times as you want. The difference is that you choose the durations — TimeTally doesn't force any particular block length.

What happens to my data? +

Everything stays in your browser. TimeTally uses localStorage — your task lists and settings never leave your device. No account, no cloud sync, no data collection.

Does TimeTally work offline? +

Yes. Once visited, you can install TimeTally as a PWA and it works fully offline. Your saved task lists are available without an internet connection.
